CELEBRATING SOLIDARITY IN "EUROPE FOR CITIZENS"

On the 6th of October 2016 we gathered partners from 5 countries and started a project „celebrating solidarity“ through which we will promote, in Serbia, Bulgaria, Albania, Macedonia, Poland and Hungary philanthropy and examples of solidarity.

Thanks to support of "Europe for Citizens Programme of the European Union", foundation and partners will celebrate, during next year, solidarity and promote good practice examples, especially in a time of crisis.
