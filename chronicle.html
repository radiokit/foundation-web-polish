What is Chronicle?

Chronicle is a system for managing your audio content. It has many faces - from a fullscreen player, through nice tiles of podcasts, to geolocated sounds on a map.

However it was designed with sound in mind - it is prepared to work as a video hosting platform as well.

By default it is integrated with social media, like facebook or twitter and with Google Analytics.

It has a flexible structure of imports & exports between other systems.
